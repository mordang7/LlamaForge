WELCOME TO LLAMAFORGE (BETA V0.1a)
================================

Thank you for downloading LlamaForge!

HOW TO USE:
1. Double-click "LlamaForge_Beta_V0.1a.exe" to launch the application.
2. The Web Interface will open automatically.
   (If not, go to http://127.0.0.1:5000 in your browser).
3. First Time Setup:
   - Go to "Server Parameters".
   - Click the Folder icon next to "Server Path".
   - Select your "llama-server.exe" file.
4. Select a Model:
   - Browse to your models folder or paste a path.
   - Click "Scan Folder".
   - Select a model and click "Start Server".

REQUIREMENTS:
- Windows 10/11
- "llama-server.exe" (from llama.cpp release)

ENJOY!
